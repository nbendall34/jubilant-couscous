New Zealand is famous for being the filming location of Lord of the Rings and the Hobbiton Movie Set. It is also famous for its Maori culture and amazing adventure activities. Bungy Jumping was invented here so you must try it here. It has superb Whitewater rafting, plenty of skydiving and jet boat tours.

Flights tend to be expensive to New Zealand, I flew with Qantas for £1,157

Qantas 2 stops 29 hr 20 m £1,157

Emirates 2 stops 27 h 55 m £1,163

Air New Zealand 2 stops 28 h 35 m £1,290

December, January and February are the busiest time of year for tourists visiting New Zealand. To ensure you get your first choice of accommodation and rental car you will need book your holiday quite far in advance.
